Why Michael Jordan > Lebron James

  1. Championships
    • Michael Jordan has 6 Championships compared to Lebum's 3.5 (Mickey Mouse Ring = .5).
    • Michael Jordan has a greater playoff win % (.665) than Lebum (.647).
    • Michael Jordan is 6-0 in the Finals while Lebum is 4-6.
  2. Skills
    • MJ being smaller and less gifted size-wise, needed more skill to gain an advantage over his oppenent. Lebum only lowers his shoulder and overpowers opponents.
    • Many Lebum Glazers constantly mention his longevity, which came from newly-developed technology. He should have many more chips than MJ in this argument. It's not the case.
    • MJ made the all NBA defensive team 9 times compared to Lebum's 0. Additionally, MJ holds the record for career point average at 30ppg.
  3. Clutch-Factor
    • MJ is known for his play in important moments such as his 31.3ppg in elimination games and his iconic game winning shots.
    • MJ never allowed a finals to go to a game 7, finishing the job early.
    • Lebum was constantly bailed out by teammates (Ray Allen, Kyrie Irving, Anthony Davis) in clutch moments.
